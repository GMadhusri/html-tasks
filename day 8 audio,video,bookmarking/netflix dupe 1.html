Hi Nanna

SEASON 1
EPISODE-1

Hi Nanna (transl. Hi Dad) is a 2023 Indian Telugu-language romantic drama film directed by Shouryuv in his directorial debut. The film stars Nani and Mrunal Thakur in the lead roles, alongside Kiara Khanna, Priyadarshi Pulikonda, Angad Bedi and Viraj Ashwin in supporting roles. The music was composed by Hesham Abdul Wahab, while the cinematography and editing were handled by Sanu Varghese and Praveen Anthony.
